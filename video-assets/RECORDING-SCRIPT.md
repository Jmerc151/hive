## CapCut Assembly Guide

### Setup
1. Open CapCut → New Project → 16:9 (1920x1080)
2. Import all screenshots and screen recordings

### Timeline Order
Drop clips in order: Shot 1 through Shot 13

### Transitions
- Between title cards: **Fade** (0.5s)
- Between screen recordings: **Cross Dissolve** (0.3s)
- Title card → screen recording: **Fade** (0.5s)

### Text Overlays
- Font: Inter or Helvetica
- Size: 24-32px
- Color: White with slight shadow
- Position: Bottom center, 80px from bottom
- Duration: Match clip length

### Music
- Search CapCut's music library for: "tech" or "minimal" or "cinematic"
- Recommended style: Low ambient electronic, builds slightly
- Volume: 30-40% (background level)

### Color
- Title cards are already styled (dark theme, gold accent)
- For screen recordings: add slight vignette (edges darker)
- Optional: CapCut filter "Film" or "Cinematic" at 20% opacity

### Export
- Resolution: 1080p
- Frame rate: 30fps
- Format: MP4
- Quality: High
